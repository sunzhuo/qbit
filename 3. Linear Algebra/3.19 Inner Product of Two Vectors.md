# Inner Product of Two Vectors

**Input:** None.

**Goal:** Return the inner product $\langle V , W \rangle$ of the following vectors:

$$V = \begin{bmatrix} -6 \\ 9i \end{bmatrix}, W = \begin{bmatrix} 3  \\ -8 \end{bmatrix}$$

## Solution Notes

$$\langle V, W \rangle = V^\dagger W = 
\begin{bmatrix}
    -6 \\
    9i
\end{bmatrix}^\dagger
\begin{bmatrix}
    3 \\
    -8
\end{bmatrix} =
\begin{bmatrix} -6 & -9i \end{bmatrix}
\begin{bmatrix}
    3 \\
    -8
\end{bmatrix}
= (-6) \cdot 3 + (-9i) \cdot (-8) = -18 + 72i$$
