# Outer Product of Two Vectors

**Input:** None.

**Goal:** Return the outer product $VW^\dagger$ of the following vectors:

$$V = \begin{bmatrix} -3i \\ 9 \end{bmatrix}, W = \begin{bmatrix} 9i \\ 2 \end{bmatrix}$$

## Solution Notes

$$\begin{bmatrix} -3i \\ 9 \end{bmatrix}
\begin{bmatrix} 9i \\ 2 \end{bmatrix}^\dagger =
\begin{bmatrix} -3i \\ 9 \end{bmatrix}
\begin{bmatrix} -9i & 2 \end{bmatrix} =
\begin{bmatrix}
    (-3i) \cdot (-9i) & (-3i) \cdot 2 \\
    9 \cdot (-9i) & 9 \cdot 2
\end{bmatrix} =
\begin{bmatrix}
    -27 & -6i \\
    -81i & 18
\end{bmatrix}$$
